About Us

Hustle for that Muscle

Datta Fitness Club is a community founded by brothers Saurabh and Siddhesh Diwase, focusing on health and well-being.
They offer a supportive environment for individuals to achieve fitness goals and promote a holistic approach to wellness through various programs.
These include blood donation camps, trekking adventures, wellness vacations, and tree plantation initiatives.
Datta Fitness Club's mission is to empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals in a supportive environment, providing state-of-the-art equipment, personalized guidance, and a diverse range of fitness programs.
